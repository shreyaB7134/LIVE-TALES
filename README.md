# 📖 Interactive Storybook

_A magical, multi-page journey powered by HTML, CSS, and JavaScript_

🌐 **[Live Demo →](https://luxury-torte-196203.netlify.app/)**

---

## ✨ Features

- 🌟 **Interactive Page Navigation** – Seamlessly flip through story pages  
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works beautifully on all devices  
- 🔊 **Optional Sound Effects** – Immerse yourself in the world with sounds  
- 🔁 **Restart Button** – Begin your adventure all over again  
- 👆 **Easy Controls** – Simple Next & Back buttons for smooth flow  

---

## 🛠 Built With

- 🧱 **HTML5**
- 🎨 **CSS3**
- ⚙️ **JavaScript (ES6)**

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

Let your imagination take the wheel. With **World Explorer Mode**, every click reveals a new chapter in a whimsical universe. Whether you're telling fairy tales or building an interactive comic, this template is ready to bring your story to life.
